My Journey

Hello! I'm Muhammad Ali, a passionate front-end developer from Pakistan with a deep love for creating immersive web experiences. My journey into web development began during my F.Sc Pre-Medical studies, where I discovered the fascinating world of coding and digital creation.

Despite my medical background, I was drawn to the logical creativity of programming. I started with basic HTML and CSS, gradually expanding my skills to include JavaScript, React, and modern web development frameworks. Each project I build is a step toward mastering the art of creating beautiful, functional, and user-friendly web applications.

My goal is to become a full-stack developer and eventually start my own freelancing career, using my skills to help businesses and individuals bring their digital visions to life. I'm particularly interested in the intersection of web development and gaming, looking for ways to incorporate gaming elements into web interfaces to create more engaging user experiences.
